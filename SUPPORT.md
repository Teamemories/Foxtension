# Support Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in this project.  
This document explains how to request help, report issues, and find answers efficiently.

Our goal is to provide a clear and structured support process so that maintainers can respond effectively and contributors can get the assistance they need.

---

## 📌 Before Requesting Support

Please review the following resources first:

- **README.md** — Installation, usage, and examples  
- **Documentation** — API reference and guides (if available)  
- **Existing Issues** — Your question may already be answered  
- **Discussions** — Community Q&A and general conversations  

If your question is already covered, please avoid opening a duplicate request.

---

## 🆘 How to Get Help

### 1. Ask a Question (General Support)
For general questions, clarifications, or usage help:

- Open a **Discussion** under:  
  **`Discussions → Q&A`**

This is the best place for:
- “How do I…?”  
- “Is this feature supported?”  
- “What’s the recommended way to…?”  

Please **do not** open an Issue for general questions.

---

### 2. Report a Bug
If you believe you’ve found a bug:

Open an **Issue** using the **Bug Report Template** and include:

- Steps to reproduce  
- Expected behavior  
- Actual behavior  
- Environment details (OS, .NET version, package version)  
- Logs or screenshots (if applicable)  

Clear and complete reports help us fix problems faster.

---

### 3. Request a Feature
For feature ideas or enhancements:

- Open an **Issue** using the **Feature Request Template**

Include:

- What problem the feature solves  
- Why it’s useful  
- Possible implementation ideas (optional)

We welcome suggestions, but we may decline features that don’t align with the project’s goals.

---

## 🔒 Security Issues

If you discover a **security vulnerability**, do **NOT** open a public Issue.

Instead, follow the instructions in:

**`SECURITY.md`**

We take security reports seriously and will respond promptly.

---

## ⏱️ Response Expectations

This project is maintained voluntarily.  
We aim to respond within:

- **48 hours** — Acknowledgment  
- **7 days** — Initial review  
- **As soon as possible** — Fixes or follow‑ups  

Response times may vary depending on workload and severity.

---

## 🤝 Community Conduct

All interactions must follow:

**`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`**

We expect respectful, constructive communication from everyone.

---

## 🙏 Thank You

Your feedback, questions, and contributions help improve this project.  
We appreciate your time and effort in making the community better.
